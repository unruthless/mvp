TK TK It costs a lot to keep the traffic of Santa Cruz flowing. If your business has a higher traffic need than your space allows it is your responsibilty to pay into a fund that helps traffic improvements. This is done by a one time charge called TIF (Traffic Impact Fee)

TIF helps pay for things like wider lanes and signalized intersections.

TIF can be $1000 to $200,000!

This is why we reccomend picking a location with a similar use to yours. If you love a space with a serious TIF you should talk to The office of Economic Development. They will try to help.
